About the Training Hub

A mathematics support website for technical trainees in Civil Engineering, Building Technology, and Land Survey.

Purpose

Mr Mwirigi Mathematics Training Hub is designed to help trainees access organized mathematics learning materials, complete assignments, and revise for assessments in engineering-related mathematics units.

Training focus

The site focuses on practical mathematics for technical training, including arithmetic, algebra, geometry, trigonometry, calculus, vectors, matrices, statistics, data analysis, coordinates, bearings, gradients, measurements, estimation, and engineering problem solving.

  • Land Survey
