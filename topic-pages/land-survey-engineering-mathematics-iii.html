Formula summary

Key formulas

The formulas below are rendered using MathJax for professional mathematical display.

Mean\[\bar{x}=\frac{\sum x}{n}\]
Standard deviation\[s=\sqrt{\frac{\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}{n-1}}\]
Probability\[P(A)=\frac{n(A)}{n(S)}\]
Matrix system\[AX=B\]
Error\[e=\mathrm{observed}-\mathrm{true}\]

Survey data collection

In land survey, this concept supports technical judgement in tasks such as repeated observations. The learner must connect the numerical answer to site, drawing, survey, measurement, or data decisions.

Core formula

Worked method

Repeated angles

Method: identify the given values, write the correct formula, substitute carefully, simplify, attach units, and interpret the result.
