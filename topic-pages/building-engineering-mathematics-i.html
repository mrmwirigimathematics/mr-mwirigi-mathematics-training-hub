Section 1

Algebra in building works

Formula substitution and transposition.

In building technology, this concept supports technical judgement in tasks such as roof pitch. The learner must connect the numerical answer to site, drawing, survey, measurement, or data decisions.

Core formula

\[y=mx+c\]

Worked method

Roof pitch

Find the roof angle when rise is 1.8 m and horizontal span is 4.2 m.

Method: identify the given values, write the correct formula, substitute carefully, simplify, attach units, and interpret the result.

Common errors

  • Using a formula before checking what each symbol means.
  • Mixing units such as mm, cm and m.
  • Rounding too early.
  • Giving a final number without technical interpretation.

Practice

  1. Create and solve one original problem involving roof pitch.
  2. Explain the formula used and define every symbol.
  3. State the answer with units and one technical interpretation.
