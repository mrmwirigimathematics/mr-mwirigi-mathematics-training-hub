Right-triangle trigonometry

Sine, cosine and tangent in construction geometry.

In civil engineering, this concept supports technical judgement in tasks such as heights. The learner must connect the numerical answer to site, drawing, survey, measurement, or data decisions.

Core formula

\[a^2+b^2=c^2\]

Worked method

Height of retaining wall

From a point 12 m away, the angle of elevation to the top of a wall is 32°. Estimate the height.

Method: identify the given values, write the correct formula, substitute carefully, simplify, attach units, and interpret the result.

Common errors

  • Using a formula before checking what each symbol means.
  • Mixing units such as mm, cm and m.

  • Giving a final number without technical interpretation.
